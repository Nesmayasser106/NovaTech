✦ How We Started

Passion for Technology

GradientTech was born in a small apartment in 2019 when our founder, frustrated with overpriced and underperforming electronics, decided to create a different kind of tech store.

Our mission was simple: curate only the best products at fair prices, backed by genuine expertise and exceptional service. We hand-test every product before adding it to our catalog.

Today, we serve customers across the country, but our core values remain unchanged — quality, transparency, and a genuine love for great technology.
